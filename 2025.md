## Online Participation

Online attendees will have Zoom access to all the live presenters. We'll also show the videos of online presenters.

Plenaries and their sign language interpreters will be recorded and those recordings will be made available.

We'll upload the links to videos in Guidebook on the day of their presentation and they will be accessible in the app long after the conference.

More on how to use Guidebook: <https://support.guidebook.com/hc/en-us/articles/360056582614-Guidebook-Web-for-End-Users>

Note: If a speaker doesn't provide their pre-recorded presentation, you won't find a video in their session details.

The Friday banquet with Freddy Boswell is NOT be broadcast on Zoom.
